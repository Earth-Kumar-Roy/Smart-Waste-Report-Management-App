##  The Problem
* **Slow & Fragmented Reporting:** Traditional complaint systems are often offline or involve tedious manual processes.
* **Lack of Accountability:** Citizens rarely receive updates, and there is no proof that a task was completed effectively.
* **Location Confusion:** Text-based addresses in crowded or unplanned areas make it difficult for field workers to locate waste hotspots.

##  The Solution
* **ğŸ“ Auto GPS Capture:** Pinpoints the exact location of the waste without requiring manual address entry.
* **ğŸ“¸ Proof-of-Work Closure:** Tickets cannot be closed until the field worker uploads a "Cleanup Photo" for verification.
* **ğŸ¯ Zone-Based Assignment:** Intelligent routing ensures workers only see tasks relevant to their specific assigned region.
* **ğŸ” Status Updates:** Transparency for citizens from the moment of submission to the final resolution.

## âš™ï¸ How It Works

### For Citizens
1. **Report:** Snap a photo of the waste and submit it (GPS is captured automatically).
2. **Track:** Receive a unique Ticket ID to monitor progress.
3. **Resolve:** Get notified once the area is cleaned.

### For Field Workers
1. **Login:** Access the worker dashboard.
2. **Navigate:** View assigned tasks in your specific zone.
3. **Clear & Verify:** Clean the spot, upload a "Post-Cleanup" photo, and close the ticket.
